Your home aboard when you're in town

The UKs first NFT Timeshare for digital nomads, remote teams and those who travel on a regular basis to work and play.

Yours for as long as you need

Our NFT tokens last for 5 years and and gives you one large ensuite room for a week (Monday-Thurs) or weekend (Friday-Sunday) per year.

The times that suit you

Your token gives you the same week or weekend each year. Pick the dates that suit you or your friends. You can trade with others to change dates.

Share with others

You can share your token with friends or others if you don't need the room or make available to other members of the community.

Bring the crew

There are 4 appartments with 3,4,4 and 5 bedrooms accordingly. Your friends and collegues can get tokens for adjacent rooms when you're there.

NFT smart contracts

Each token is backed with a smart contract on the Etherium blockchain. Trade or sell as you would with any NFT.

Renewals

When your token expires you can renew for the same price you paid for it. If the market values them higher - then it's yours to sell to the highest bidder.
