Formally an oil tanker, now an sanctuary

Our tanker used to travel the Thames delivering oil. Now converted to sustainable accommodation and an amazing place to stay.

The vessel has been converted into 4 apartments with 16 bedrooms in total. There are 3 large communal areas in the forward appartments, and in the rear apartment the communal space is in the converted engine room.

  • The large studio bedrooms are in the former oil tanks. Each with ensuite bathrooms and mezzanine beds.
  • There are 2 outdoor areas, the bow and a the spacious deck with planting and hammocks.
  • Each apartment has a kitchen and dining area.

Apartment 1

Apartment 1 is a 3 studio apartment at the front of the vessel.
It includes a large living area with kitchen and wood burning stove.
There are 2 large ensuite studios with mezzanine beds, desks, wardrobes and plenty of storage. These studios could have an additional single bed for families. The third studio is the former bow locker of the vessel.

This is a very large room, with portholes, a bed on a platform and a jacuzzi bath and steam shower.

Apartment 2

Flat 2 is a large 4 studio apartment with generous living and working area, shared kitchen and wood burning stove.

The 4 studios are the same sizes, all with mezzanine beds, ensuite pod bathrooms, desks and wardrobes.

Apartment 3

Flat 2 is a large 4 studio apartment with generous living and working area, shared kitchen and wood burning stove.

There are 4 studios all with mezzanine beds, ensuite pod bathrooms, desks and wardrobes.

2 of the rooms have beds that are waist height rather than mezzanies.

Apartment 4

Flat 4 consists of the former crews quarters and engine room.

The engine room has been converted into an amazing entertaining space with spiral staircase made from the engine room floor, a suspended wood burning stove made from the engine compressor and many original features.

There is a stage area and large kitchen for social occasions.
